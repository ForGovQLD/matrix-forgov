About

As well as being a month-long celebration and recognition of how far we have come since the legalisation of same sex marriage on 9 December 2017, Pride Month is also an opportunity to peacefully raise awareness of current issues facing the LGBTIQ+ community.

Look out for opportunities to link into existing events such as panel discussions and online forums, and participate in rainbow-themed days and events. Think about how you can raise awareness of the history and importance of Pride Month, and further promote how you are supporting LGBTIQ+ employees in your agency. We support our employees. #BeYou
